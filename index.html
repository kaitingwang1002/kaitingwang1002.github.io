Chronology of WANG Kaiting
2001, Born In the evening of October 2nd, Tuesday, August 16th in the lunar calendar, a baby boy was born in Beijing. 2008, Age 7 In September, he entered Gonghua Central Primary School (巩华中心小学) in Changping District, Beijing. 2014, Age 13 He began attending Wenhui Middle School (北京市文汇中学) in Dongcheng District, Beijing. His homeroom teacher was Mrs. CHEN Jian. 2017, Age 16 He was admitted to Beijing No.5 High School (北京市第五中学) for senior high school studies. His homeroom teacher was Mr. YANG Zheng. 2020, Age 19 He was admitted to China University of Petroleum-Beijing (中国石油大学（北京）). One year later, he transferred into the major of Machine Design, Manufacturing and Automation. His academic advisor was Mr. WANG Wenjie. 2024, Age 23 Under the guidance of Special Associate Prof. WANG Tun, his undergraduate thesis titled "Research and Design of Water-Cooling System for Directly Driven Top-Drive Main Bearing" won an national award and he obtained a Bachelor of Engineering (BEng) degree. Later this year, he enrolled in the Mechanical Engineering program at City University of Hong Kong (香港城市大學). His research focused on the application of MXene materials in supercapacitors, under the supervision of Prof. ZHANG Kaili and Pakistani scholar Dr. HUSSAIN Iftikhar. 2025, Age 24 He graduated from City University of Hong Kong with a Master of Science (MSc) in Mechanical Engineering, achieving Distinction and receiving the Dr. & Mrs. Yeung Kin Man Scholarship (100,000 HKD). In the second half of the year, he enrolled at Western University (University of Western Ontario), Canada, to pursue a PhD in Mechanical and Materials Engineering under the supervision of Dr. HaoTian Harvey Shi. His research focuses on the development of micro and nano-structured electrode materials for smart textile applications.
