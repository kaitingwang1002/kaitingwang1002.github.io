Chronology of WANG Kaiting
2001, Born
In the evening of October 2nd, Tuesday, August 16th in the lunar calendar, a baby boy was born in Beijing.
2008, Age 7
In September, he entered 巩华中心小学 (Gonghua Central Primary School) in Changping District, Beijing.
2014, Age 13
He began attending 北京市文汇中学 (Wenhui Middle School) in Dongcheng District, Beijing. His homeroom teacher was Mrs. CHEN Jian.
2017, Age 16
He was admitted to 北京市第五中学 (Beijing No.5 High School) for senior high school studies. His homeroom teacher was Mr. YANG Zheng.
2020, Age 19
He was admitted to 中国石油大学（北京） (China University of Petroleum-Beijing). One year later, he transferred into the major of Machine Design, Manufacturing and Automation. His academic advisor was Mr. WANG Wenjie.
2024, Age 23
Under the guidance of Special Associate Professor WANG Tun, his undergraduate thesis titled “Research and Design of Water-Cooling System for Directly Driven Top-Drive Main Bearing” won an award. Later this year, he enrolled in the Mechanical Engineering program at 香港城市大學 (City University of Hong Kong). His research focused on the application of MXene materials in supercapacitors, under the supervision of Professor ZHANG Kaili and Pakistani scholar Dr. HUSSAIN Iftikhar.
